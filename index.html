🌿 Tips to Manage Dementia

  • 🏃‍♂️ Exercise daily to keep your brain active
  • 🧩 Play puzzles and memory games
  • 🥗 Eat healthy food
  • 😴 Get proper sleep
  • 👨‍👩‍👧 Stay connected with family
  • 🧘 Practice meditation

✨ Stay Positive & Strong
